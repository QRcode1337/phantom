# Phantom GUI: Vision & Roadmap

## Vision

A specialized, high-performance quantitative dashboard tracking chaos scores and regime shifts across Weather, Price, and ARGUS domains. The interface should feel like a state-of-the-art terminal for a quantitative researcher, prioritizing data density and clarity.
